Batibot was a Philippine children's television series produced by PCTV and based on "Sesame Street". It was first aired in 1984 on RPN as "Sesame!" and was co-produced by Children's Television Workshop ("now known as" Sesame Workshop) but the partnership broke up. "Sesame!" later aired as "Batibot" in 1985, a full Filipino language series. It aired until 1998 and was aired in at least four television networks.
