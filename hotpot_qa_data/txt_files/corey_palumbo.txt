Corey Lee Palumbo (born August 16, 1972) is a West Virginia State Senator representing the 17th district in the West Virginia Senate. A state senator since 2009, he previously served as a member of the West Virginia House of Delegates from 2003 to 2009. He is the son of Mario Palumbo, the 32nd Attorney General of West Virginia as well as a former five-term state senator.
