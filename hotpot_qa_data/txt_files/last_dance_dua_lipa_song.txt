"Last Dance" is a song by English singer Dua Lipa from her eponymous debut studio album (2017). Lipa wrote the song with Stephen "Koz" Kozmeniuk and Talay Riley, with Kozmeniuk handling the song's production. It was released on 9 February 2016 as the album's second single. The track appears on the deluxe edition of "Dua Lipa". It peaked at number four on the "Billboard" Twitter Emerging Artists chart, spending seven weeks on it.
