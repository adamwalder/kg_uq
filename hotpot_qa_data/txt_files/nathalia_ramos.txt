Nathalia Norah Ramos Cohen (born July 3, 1992) is a Spanish-Australian actress, who also holds U.S. citizenship. Ramos is known for her portrayals of Yasmin in the 2007 film "", Jill in the 2013 film "The Damned", and lead character Nina Martin in the 2011 Nickelodeon television series "House of Anubis".
