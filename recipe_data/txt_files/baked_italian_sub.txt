The ingredients for making baked italian sub are (2, 7- to 8-inch, hoagie rolls), (6, thin slices, deli ham), (6, thin slices, Genoa salami), (6, thin slices, capicola ham), (2, slices, provolone cheese, halved), (1/3, cup, sliced, banana peppers), (to taste, tomato slices), (to taste, shredded lettuce), (to taste, thinly sliced red onion), (drizzle of, olive oil), (drizzle of, red wine vinegar), (1, pinch, oregano), (salt, and, freshly ground black pepper).
To make a Baked Italian Sub, preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Slice hoagie rolls in half lengthwise and place them on a baking sheet. Layer with deli ham, Genoa salami, capicola ham, provolone cheese, banana peppers, tomato slices, lettuce, red onion. Drizzle with olive oil, red wine vinegar, sprinkle with oregano, salt, and pepper. Bake in the oven until cheese is melted and bread is toasted.
