The ingredients for making banana cream pie are The list of ingredients for banana cream pie, with measurements, is as follows: Sugar (white), Flour (all-purpose), Salt (a pinch), Milk (2 cups), Egg yolks (3), Butter, Vanilla, Pie shell (store-bought pastry crust or make your own at home), Bananas..
To make banana cream pie, combine dry ingredients in a saucepan and stir in milk. Bring to a boil and cook until thick. Whisk some of the milk mixture into egg yolks, then whisk the egg mixture into the saucepan. Cook for two more minutes, remove from heat, and stir in butter and vanilla. Place banana slices in a pie shell, then pour the milk mixture over the bananas. Bake until the filling is set..
