The ingredients for making beef and green bean stir fry are 1 pound, sirloin, sirloin tip, sliced thinly across the grain, 1 1/2 teaspoons, baking soda, 3 tablespoons, cornstarch, 1/3 cup, water, 1 teaspoon, sugar, 6 tablespoons, soy sauce, 1 1/2 tablespoons, Shaoxing, wine, 1/4 teaspoon, Chinese 5-spice powder, 1/8 teaspoon, ground white pepper, 2 tablespoons, vegetable oil, 2/3 cup, vertically sliced onion, 1/2 cup, matchstick-cut carrots, 1 (1 pound) package frozen French-cut green beans, 4 cloves garlic, minced, 1 (2-inch) piece fresh ginger, peeled and grated, 1 cup beef stock or broth, 1 teaspoon, sesame oil, 2 cups hot cooked rice or cauliflower rice, 1 teaspoon, sesame seeds.
To make beef and green bean stir fry, start by marinating thinly sliced sirloin in a mixture of baking soda, cornstarch, water, sugar, soy sauce, Shaoxing wine, Chinese 5-spice powder, and white pepper. In a hot skillet, stir-fry onions, carrots, green beans, garlic, and ginger. Add the marinated beef and cook until browned. Pour in beef stock and sesame oil, then simmer until the sauce thickens. Serve over cooked rice and garnish with sesame seeds if desired. Enjoy!The following is preparation information for beef and green bean stir fry, 25 mins, 10 mins, 35 mins, N/A, 4 servings, 2 cups.
