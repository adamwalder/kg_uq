To make bonbons, start by making the filling with butter, confectioners' sugar, sweetened condensed milk, coconut flakes, walnuts, and vanilla. Chill the filling for at least two hours. Roll the filling into balls and freeze until hard. Melt chocolate chips and shortening together in a double boiler. Dip each bonbon in the melted chocolate and place on wax paper to dry. Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to five days or freeze for up to three months..
