The following is preparation information for brownie pie, prep time: 30 mins, cook time: 45 mins, total time: 2 hrs 15 mins, servings: 6, yield: 1 brownie pie.
The ingredients for making brownie pie are 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour, plus more for rolling, 1 tablespoon granulated sugar, ½ teaspoon kosher salt, ½ cup cold unsalted butter (cut into 1/2-inch pieces), 3 tablespoons ice water, 10 tablespoons unsalted butter (melted and cooled), ¾ cup granulated sugar, 2 large eggs (at room temperature), 1 large egg yolk (at room temperature), 2 tablespoons canola oil, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder, ½ cup all-purpose flour, 2 teaspoons instant espresso granules, ½ teaspoon kosher salt, ½ cup chopped walnuts or hazelnuts, ½ cup semisweet chocolate chips..
To make brownie pie, start by making the crust. Combine flour, sugar, and salt in a food processor. Add cold butter and pulse until it resembles pea-sized pieces. Drizzle water over the mixture. Press the crust into a pie dish and chill for 30 minutes. 

For the filling, mix melted butter, sugar, eggs, egg yolk, canola oil, and vanilla extract. Add cocoa powder, flour, espresso granules, and salt. Stir in chopped nuts and half of the chocolate chips. Pour the filling into the chilled crust. Sprinkle the remaining chocolate chips on top. 

Bake the pie for 45 minutes. Let it cool before serving with ice cream, whipped cream, and salted caramel..
