The ingredients for making caramel cake are The list of ingredients for caramel cake is as follows: white sugar, butter, eggs, flour, baking powder, salt, milk, vanilla extract, brown sugar, evaporated milk, confectioners' sugar..
To make caramel cake, start by preheating the oven to 350 degrees F and greasing and flouring three 9-inch cake pans. In a mixing bowl, beat white sugar and butter until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking powder, and salt. Add the flour mixture to the butter mixture in batches, alternating with milk, and beat until the batter makes ribbons. Divide the batter among the prepared cake pans and bake for about 30 minutes. Let the cakes cool for 5 minutes, then invert them onto a cooling rack and let them cool for another 30 minutes. For the caramel icing, combine brown sugar, butter, and salt in a saucepan over medium heat until the sugar is dissolved. Stir in evaporated milk and let it boil for 4 minutes. Remove from heat and let it cool for 5 minutes. Mix in confectioners' sugar and vanilla extract until the icing caramelizes and thickens. Spread the icing onto the cooled cake layers and stack them. Ice the top and sides of the cake..
