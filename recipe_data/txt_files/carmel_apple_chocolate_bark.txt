The ingredients for making carmel apple chocolate bark are chocolate, caramel bits, pretzels, pecans, apple chips.
To make caramel apple chocolate bark, melt 2 cups of chocolate chips in the microwave and spread onto a lined baking sheet. Drizzle melted caramel on top and swirl with a spatula. Add crushed pretzels, chopped pecans, and apple chips. Press toppings into the caramel and chill for 15 minutes. Drizzle with melted chocolate and refrigerate for 2-3 hours. Cut into squares or break into pieces to serve..
