The ingredients for making carrot rasin salad are The ingredients for Carrot Raisin Salad are:
- 4 cups shredded carrots
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon white sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt.
Combine shredded carrots, crushed pineapple (optional), raisins, sugar, mayonnaise, and freshly squeezed lemon juice in a large bowl; mix well. Refrigerate for 2 hours before serving for flavors to meld for best results.The following is preparation information for carrot rasin salad, Prep Time: 20 mins, Chill Time: 2 hrs, Total Time: 2 hrs 20 mins, Servings: 12.
The nutritional content for carrot rasin salad consists of 169 Calories, 8g Fat, 27g Carbs, 1g Protein.
