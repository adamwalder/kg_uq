The ingredients for making chef john's chicken tortilla soup are 1 (4 1/2 pound) whole chicken, 3 white onions, halved, divided, 1 bunch cilantro, rinsed well, 2 bay leaves, 1/2 teaspoon whole black peppercorns, 1 teaspoon Mexican oregano, 2 dried chili pepper pods, seeded (optional), 3 quarts water, 12 corn tortillas, 1/4 cup vegetable oil, 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt, 3 large tomatoes, 1 sweet red pepper, halved, seeded, 1 large, 2 small jalapeno peppers, halved, seeded, 6 garlic cloves, peeled, 1 whole canned chipotle pepper in adobo sauce (optional), 1 teaspoon ground cumin, 1 lime, juiced..
To make Chef John's Chicken Tortilla Soup, start by combining a whole chicken, onions, cilantro, bay leaves, peppercorns, oregano, dried chili pods, and water in a large soup pot. Simmer until the chicken is cooked. In a separate pan, char tomatoes, red pepper, jalapeno peppers, garlic, and chipotle pepper. Blend the charred vegetables with cumin and lime juice. Shred the cooked chicken and add it back to the broth. Serve the soup topped with tortilla strips, charred vegetable salsa, sour cream, avocado, cilantro, Mexican cheese, lime wedges, and cayenne pepper.
