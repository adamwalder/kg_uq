The ingredients for making chicken zoodle soup in a jar are 4 teaspoons, chicken stock base, 2 cloves, garlic, minced, 1/2 teaspoon, dried rosemary, 1/2 teaspoon, ground sage, 1/4 teaspoon, freshly, ground black pepper, 1 2/3 cups, shredded, cooked chicken, 2 1/2 cups, spiralized, zucchini, 1/2 cup, shredded carrot, 1/2 cup, thinly sliced, celery, 1/3 cup, finely, chopped sweet onion.
To make chicken zoodle soup in a jar, start by stirring together chicken stock base, garlic, dried rosemary, ground sage, and black pepper in a small bowl. Then, layer shredded cooked chicken, spiralized zucchini, shredded carrot, thinly sliced celery, and finely chopped sweet onion in a jar. When ready to eat, pour hot water over the ingredients in the jar, cover, and let it sit for a few minutes before stirring and enjoying.The following is preparation information for chicken zoodle soup in a jar, Prep Time: 10 mins, Total Time: 10 mins, Servings: 3.
