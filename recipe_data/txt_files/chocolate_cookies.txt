The nutritional content for chocolate cookies consists of 116 Calories, 7g Fat, 14g Carbs, 2g Protein.
The following is preparation information for chocolate cookies, prep time: 15 mins, cook time: 10 mins, total time: 25 mins, servings: 48, yield: 48 cookies.
The ingredients for making chocolate cookies are sugar (2 cups), margarine (or butter), eggs (2), vanilla extract, all-purpose flour, unsweetened cocoa powder, baking soda, salt, nuts (walnuts or pecans).
To make chocolate cookies, cream sugar and margarine, beat in eggs and vanilla. Combine dry ingredients and add to the wet mixture. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto cookie sheets and bake. Store cooled cookies in an airtight container at room temperature. You can also freeze baked cookies or cookie dough for up to two months..
