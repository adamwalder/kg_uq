The ingredients for making chocolate stout cake are The list of ingredients for chocolate stout cake is as follows: 
- 2 cups dark chocolate chips (such as Ghriradelli® 60% Cacao Bittersweet Chocolate Chips)
- 1 1/2 cups white sugar
- 1/4 cup water
- 3 tablespoons light corn syrup
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 3 tablespoons Irish cream liqueur (such as Bailey's®)
- 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
- 1 (11.2 ounce) bottle stout beer (such as Guinness™)
- 1 tablespoon dark brown sugar
- 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 cup cocoa powder (such as Hershey's® Special Dark)
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter
- 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 cup dark brown sugar
- 1/2 cup white sugar
- 2 large eggs, at room temperature
- 1 cup full-fat sour cream, at room temperature
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract.
To make chocolate stout cake, start by greasing and flouring two 9-inch round cake pans. In a separate bowl, sift together flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. In a large bowl or stand mixer, beat butter, vegetable oil, dark brown sugar, and white sugar until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Mix in sour cream and vanilla extract. Gradually add in the dry ingredients, alternating with the cooled stout mixture. Divide the batter between the prepared cake pans and bake in a preheated oven for 30 to 35 minutes. Allow the cakes to cool before removing from the pans. Once cooled, place one cake layer on a serving platter and spread frosting on top. Place the second cake layer on top and frost the entire cake..
