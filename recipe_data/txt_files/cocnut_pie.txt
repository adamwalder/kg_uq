The nutritional content for cocnut pie consists of 405 Calories, 24g Fat, 43g Carbs, 5g Protein.
The following is preparation information for cocnut pie, prep time: 10 mins, cook time: 1 hr, total time: 1 hr 10 mins, servings: 8, yield: 1 (9-inch) pie.
The ingredients for making cocnut pie are white sugar, all-purpose flour, eggs, butter, milk, vanilla extract, flaked coconut, unbaked pie crust.
To make coconut pie, preheat the oven to 300 degrees F. Mix sugar, flour, eggs, and softened butter in a large bowl. Stir in milk, vanilla, and flaked coconut. Pour the mixture into an unbaked pie crust. Bake for 1 hour and cool before slicing..
