The ingredients for making creamy chicken vegetable soup are 2 tablespoons, unsalted butter, 1 onion, chopped, 2 carrots, peeled, chopped, 2 stalks, celery, sliced, 1 1/2 tablespoons, all-purpose flour, 4 cups, chicken broth, 2 cups, chopped, cooked chicken, 1 large, potato, peeled, cubed, 1/3 cup, frozen peas, 1/3 cup, frozen, corn kernels, 1 teaspoon, dried parsley flakes, 1/2 teaspoon, dried marjoram, 1/2 teaspoon, dried thyme, 1/2 teaspoon, salt, 1/4 teaspoon, freshly, ground black pepper, 1/2 cup, half-and-half.
To make creamy chicken vegetable soup, start by melting butter in a large soup pot and cooking chopped onion, carrots, and celery for 5 minutes. Stir in flour, then add chicken broth, cooked chicken, potato, peas, corn, parsley, marjoram, thyme, salt, and black pepper. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 25 minutes until vegetables are tender. Stir in half-and-half and simmer for an additional 3 minutes.
