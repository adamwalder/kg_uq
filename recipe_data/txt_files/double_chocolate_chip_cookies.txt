The ingredients for making double chocolate chip cookies are sugar, butter, eggs, vanilla, cocoa powder
To make double chocolate chip cookies, follow these tips:
- Use tablespoonfuls of cookie dough for soft and chewy cookies.
- You can use butter instead of margarine.
- Consider reducing the sugar if you prefer less sweetness.
- Chill the dough before baking to prevent excessive spreading.
- White chocolate chips can be used for a tasty contrast.
- Feel free to add other ingredients like peanut butter chips, M&M's, chocolate Rice Krispies, or marshmallows.
