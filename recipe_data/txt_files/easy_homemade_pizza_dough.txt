The ingredients for making easy homemade pizza dough are Yeast, Sugar, Bread Flour, Olive Oil, Salt, Warm water.
To make easy homemade pizza dough, start by mixing warm water with yeast and sugar until creamy. Add flour, oil, and salt, then beat until smooth. Let the dough rest for 5 minutes before rolling it into a 12-inch circle on a floured surface. Transfer the dough to a pizza pan, add sauce and toppings, then bake at 450 degrees F until golden brown. Let it cool for 5 minutes before serving.
