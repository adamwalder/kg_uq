The ingredients for making egg drop soup are 1 cup chicken broth, ¼ teaspoon soy sauce, ¼ teaspoon sesame oil, 2 teaspoons water, 1 teaspoon cornstarch, 1 large egg, beaten, 1 drop yellow food coloring, ½ teaspoon ground white pepper, ⅛ teaspoon salt, 1 teaspoon chopped fresh chives.
To make egg drop soup, combine chicken broth, soy sauce, and sesame oil in a saucepan over medium heat and bring to a boil. Slowly pour in beaten egg while stirring, season with salt and white pepper, and garnish with chopped chives. Optional: add cornstarch slurry, yellow food coloring, and adjust seasonings to taste. Serve hot.The following is preparation information for egg drop soup, 5 mins, 10 mins, 15 mins, N/A, 1, N/A.
