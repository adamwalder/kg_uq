The following is preparation information for english toffee, prep time: 5 mins, cook time: 15 mins, total time: 20 mins, servings: 16.
The ingredients for making english toffee are The list of ingredients for English toffee is as follows: 1 cup unsalted butter, 1 ¼ cups white sugar, 2 tablespoons water, ¼ cup slivered almonds, 1 cup chocolate chips..
To make English toffee, butter a 10x15-inch jellyroll pan. Melt butter in a heavy skillet over medium heat. Stir in sugar and water, then bring to a boil. Add almonds and cook, stirring constantly, until nuts are toasted and sugar is golden. Pour mixture into the prepared pan; do not spread. Immediately sprinkle chocolate chips onto hot sugar mixture. Spread chocolate over the surface as it melts. Let cool completely before breaking into pieces..
