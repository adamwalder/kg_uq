The ingredients for making ginger veggie stir fry are 4 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided, 2 teaspoons chopped fresh ginger root, divided, 1 ½ cloves garlic, crushed, 1 tablespoon cornstarch, 1 small head broccoli, cut into florets, ¾ cup julienned carrots, ½ cup snow peas, ½ cup halved green beans, 2 ½ tablespoons water, 2 tablespoons soy sauce, ¼ cup chopped onion, ½ tablespoon salt.
To make ginger veggie stir-fry, start by mixing vegetable oil, ginger, garlic, and cornstarch in a bowl. Toss broccoli, carrots, snow peas, and green beans in the mixture. Cook in a skillet with oil, then add water, soy sauce, onion, salt, and more ginger. Cook until vegetables are tender but crisp. Serve over steamed jasmine rice.The following is preparation information for ginger veggie stir fry, 25 mins, 15 mins, 40 mins, N/A, 6, N/A.
