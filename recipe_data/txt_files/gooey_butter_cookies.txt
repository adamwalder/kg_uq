The nutritional content for gooey butter cookies consists of 142 calories, 8g fat, 16g carbs, 2g protein.
The following is preparation information for gooey butter cookies, prep time: 5 mins, cook time: 15 mins, total time: 20 mins, servings: 24, yield: 2 dozen.
The ingredients for making gooey butter cookies are The list of ingredients for gooey butter cookies is as follows: 
- ¼ cup confectioners' sugar 
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened 
- ½ cup unsalted butter, softened 
- 1 large egg 
- ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract 
- 1 (15.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix.
To make gooey butter cookies, preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. In a medium bowl, beat softened cream cheese and butter until creamy. Stir in an egg and vanilla extract. Add yellow cake mix and mix until well blended. Roll the dough into 1-inch balls and coat them in confectioners' sugar. Place the balls 1 inch apart on an ungreased cookie sheet. Bake for 10 to 13 minutes until set. Transfer to wire racks to cool..
