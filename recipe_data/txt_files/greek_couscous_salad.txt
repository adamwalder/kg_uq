The ingredients for making greek couscous salad are 1/2 cup water, 1/4 cup chicken broth, 1 teaspoon minced garlic, 1/2 cup pearl (Israeli) couscous, 1 cup canned chickpeas (garbanzo beans), rinsed and drained, 1/4 cup chopped sun-dried tomatoes, 1/4 cup sliced Kalamata olives, 2 tablespoons crumbled feta cheese, 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar, 1 1/2 teaspoons lemon juice, 1 teaspoon dried oregano, 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper.
To make Greek couscous salad, start by boiling water and chicken broth with minced garlic. Add pearl couscous, cover, and let it stand until water is absorbed. Fluff with a fork and let it cool. Then, mix in chickpeas, sun-dried tomatoes, Kalamata olives, feta cheese, white wine vinegar, lemon juice, dried oregano, and black pepper. Serve and enjoy!The following is preparation information for greek couscous salad, 20 mins, 5 mins, 45 mins, 20 mins, 3, 3.
