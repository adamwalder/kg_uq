The nutritional content for healthier apple crisp consists of 280 calories, 12g fat, 44g carbs, 3g protein.
The following is preparation information for healthier apple crisp, prep time, cook time, total time, additional time, servings, yield.
The ingredients for making healthier apple crisp are sliced apples, white sugar, 1 tablespoon flour, ground cinnamon, water, oats, 1 cup flour, brown sugar, baking powder, baking soda, walnuts, melted butter.
To make healthier apple crisp, place sliced apples in a 9x13-inch pan. Mix white sugar, 1 tablespoon flour, and ground cinnamon together; sprinkle over apples. Pour water evenly over all. Combine oats, 1 cup flour, brown sugar, baking powder, baking soda, walnuts, and melted butter together. Crumble evenly over the apple mixture. Bake in the preheated oven at 45 minutes or until golden and crisp on top..
