The nutritional content for healthy chocolate pudding consists of 142 Calories, 2g Fat, 31g Carbs, 2g Protein.
The following is preparation information for healthy chocolate pudding, prep time: 5 mins, cook time: 10 mins, total time: 15 mins, servings: 2, yield: 1 cup.
The ingredients for making healthy chocolate pudding are almond milk, coconut sugar, cocoa powder, cornstarch, vanilla extract.
To make healthy chocolate pudding, place almond milk, coconut sugar, cocoa powder, cornstarch, and vanilla extract in a saucepan over low heat. Stir together until thick and smooth, about 10 minutes..
