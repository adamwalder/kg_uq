The ingredients for making lemon shortbread cookies are butter, confectioners' sugar, lemon juice, flour, melted butter, lemon zest, confectioners' sugar, lemon juice.
To make lemon shortbread cookies, beat butter until creamy. Add confectioners' sugar, lemon juice, and flour mixture; mix well. Roll out dough to 1/4-inch thickness and cut into rounds. Bake at 350 degrees F for 10 minutes. Let cookies cool, then whisk melted butter, lemon juice, lemon zest, and confectioners' sugar together for glaze. Dip cookies in glaze and let set for 2 hours. Store in an airtight container..
