The ingredients for making no yeast pizza crust are Flour, Baking powder, Salt, Milk, Olive oil.
To make a no-yeast pizza crust, start by mixing all-purpose flour, baking powder, salt, fat-free milk, and olive oil until a dough forms. Knead the dough, shape it into a ball, and let it rise. Roll the dough out into a circle. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F, bake the crust for 8 minutes, then add your favorite toppings and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until golden brown. Store any leftover dough in the refrigerator for up to three days.The following is preparation information for no yeast pizza crust, 15 mins, 8 mins, 25 mins, 10 mins, 8, 1 12-inch pizza crust.
