The ingredients for making peanut butter pie are The ingredients for peanut butter pie are: 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, ½ cup creamy peanut butter, ½ cup confectioners' sugar, 1 (16 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, 1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust, and 15 miniature chocolate-covered peanut butter cups..
To make peanut butter pie, mix cream cheese, peanut butter, and confectioners' sugar until smooth. Fold in half of the whipped topping and spoon the mixture into a graham cracker crust. Spread the remaining whipped topping over the peanut butter mixture and garnish with peanut butter cups. Chill for at least 2 hours before serving..
