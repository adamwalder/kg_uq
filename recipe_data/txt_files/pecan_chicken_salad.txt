The ingredients for making pecan chicken salad are mayonnaise, ½ cup, plain Greek yogurt, ½ cup, white wine vinegar, 2 teaspoons, garlic powder, ½ teaspoon, dried thyme, ¼ teaspoon, Park Hill maple pepper (such as Savory Spice Shop), 1/2 teaspoon, chopped cooked chicken, 2 cups, celery, 2 stalks, chopped toasted pecans, ⅓ cup, minced red onion, 2 tablespoons.
To make pecan chicken salad, mix mayonnaise, Greek yogurt, white wine vinegar, garlic powder, dried thyme, and maple pepper in a bowl. Add chopped cooked chicken, sliced celery, toasted pecans, and minced red onion; stir well.
