The ingredients for making pepperoni pizza grilled cheese are 2 tablespoons, unsalted butter, 1/2 teaspoon, Italian seasoning, 1/8 teaspoon, garlic powder, 4 slices, white bread, 2 tablespoons, pizza sauce, 12 slices, pepperoni, 1/4 cup, sliced black olives, 1/2 cup, shredded mozzarella cheese.
To make pepperoni pizza grilled cheese, start by melting butter with Italian seasoning and garlic powder. Spread this mixture on bread slices. Add pizza sauce, pepperoni, black olives, and mozzarella on the bread. Cook the sandwiches in a skillet until golden and cheese is melted. Cut in half and serve immediately.The following is preparation information for pepperoni pizza grilled cheese, 10 mins, 10 mins, 20 mins, None, 2 servings, None.
The nutritional content for pepperoni pizza grilled cheese consists of 422 Calories, 25g Fat, 35g Carbs, 14g Protein.
