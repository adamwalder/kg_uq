The nutritional content for perfect pumpkin pie consists of 379 Calories, 14g Fat, 31g Carbs, 6g Protein.
The following is preparation information for perfect pumpkin pie, prep time, cook time, total time, additional time, servings, yield.
The ingredients for making perfect pumpkin pie are pumpkin puree (1 can), Eagle Brand Sweetened Condensed Milk (1 can), eggs (2 large), ground cinnamon (1 teaspoon), ground ginger (½ teaspoon), ground nutmeg (½ teaspoon), salt (½ teaspoon), unbaked pie crust (1, 9 inch).
To make the perfect pumpkin pie, make the filling, pour it into the crust, and bake for 15 minutes. Then, reduce the heat and continue baking. Let the pie cool completely before slicing and serve with homemade whipped cream. Optionally, blind bake the pie crust before adding the filling. Use canned pumpkin, not pumpkin pie filling..
