The ingredients for making pizza grilled cheese sandwich are 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese, 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, 2 tablespoons pizza sauce, 2 teaspoons mayonnaise, 2 slices bread.
To make a pizza grilled cheese sandwich, mix shredded mozzarella cheese, fresh basil leaves, and pizza sauce in a bowl. Spread mayonnaise on each slice of bread and place them mayo-side-down in a hot skillet. Spread the cheese mixture on one slice of bread and cook until golden brown and cheese melts. Place the second slice of bread on top, grilled side up. Enjoy the sandwich!The following is preparation information for pizza grilled cheese sandwich, 5 mins, 5 mins, 10 mins, N/A, 1 serving, N/A.
