The ingredients for making pork stir fry are 5 tablespoons, reduced-sodium soy sauce, 2 tablespoons, rice wine vinegar, 1 tablespoon, cornstarch, 2 tablespoons, sesame oil, divided, 1 (1 pound), pork tenderloin, cut into strips, 1 fresh red chile pepper, chopped, 2 cloves, garlic, minced, 1 onion, chopped, 1 green bell pepper, chopped, 1 head, bok choy, leaves, stalks separated, chopped, 2 crowns, broccoli, chopped, 1 teaspoon, ground ginger.
To make pork stir fry, start by whisking together soy sauce, vinegar, and cornstarch in a small bowl. Then, cook pork tenderloin strips in sesame oil until browned, set aside. In the same wok, cook red chile pepper and garlic. Add onion, bell pepper, bok choy, broccoli, and ginger. Stir in the soy sauce mixture and return the pork to the wok. Cook until heated through and serve.The following is preparation information for pork stir fry, 25 mins, 15 mins, 40 mins, N/A, 6, N/A.
