The ingredients for making shrimp and pepper stir fry are 1/2 cup chicken broth, 1/4 cup low-sodium soy sauce, 2 tablespoons rice wine vinegar, 1 tablespoon brown sugar, 1 tablespoon cornstarch, 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder, 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper, 2 tablespoons peanut oil, 1 red bell pepper, 1 yellow bell pepper, 1 red onion, 8 ounces snow peas, 6 green onions, 1 1/2 pounds large shrimp.
To make shrimp and pepper stir-fry, start by whisking together chicken broth, soy sauce, rice wine vinegar, brown sugar, cornstarch, garlic powder, and crushed red pepper in a small bowl.
