The ingredients for making sloppy joes are Ground beef, 1 pound
Onions, 1/2 cup chopped
Tomato-based sauce, 1 cup.
To make Sloppy Joes, start by cooking ground beef, onion, and green pepper. Once cooked, stir in the remaining ingredients and simmer for about 30 minutes. Serve the mixture on hamburger buns, making sure to overfill the bun so that some meat spills out for added flavor. Enjoy with potato chips on the side for a classic Sloppy Joe experience.The following is preparation information for sloppy joes, 5 mins, 30 mins, 35 mins, N/A, 6, 6 sandwiches.
