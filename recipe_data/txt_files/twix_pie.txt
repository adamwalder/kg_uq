The ingredients for making twix pie are The list of ingredients for Twix Pie, with measurements and the words 'or' and 'and' replaced with commas, is as follows:

- Cookie crumbs, 2 cups
- Melted butter, 1/2 cup
- Twix spread, 1 jar
- Cream cheese, 8 ounces
- Cream, 1 cup
- Powdered sugar, 1/2 cup
- Whipped cream, 1 cup (reserve 1 cup for topping)
- Caramel topping
- Chopped Twix bars.
To make Twix pie, stir together cookie crumbs and melted butter, then press the mixture into a deep-dish 9-inch pie plate. Bake until slightly golden brown and let cool. Beat Twix spread and cream cheese until smooth. In a separate bowl, beat cream and powdered sugar until stiff peaks form. Reserve 1 cup of whipped cream and fold the rest into the Twix spread mixture. Pour the Twix mixture into the cooled crust. Top with reserved whipped cream and chill for at least 2 hours. Drizzle with caramel topping and sprinkle with chopped Twix bars..
