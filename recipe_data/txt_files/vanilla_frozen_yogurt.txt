To make vanilla frozen yogurt, stir together 3 cups of nonfat Greek yogurt, 2/3 cup of white sugar, and 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract in a bowl until the sugar is dissolved. Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour. Pour the chilled mixture into an ice cream maker and freeze according to the manufacturer's directions until it reaches "soft-serve" consistency. Transfer to a plastic container with a lid, cover the surface with plastic wrap, and seal. Allow the frozen yogurt to ripen in the freezer for at least 2 hours or overnight..
